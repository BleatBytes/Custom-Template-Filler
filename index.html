1) Get your template ready! Any text you'd like to be replaced should be surrounded by brackets, [[like so]]. The brackets can have letters, numbers, and spaces between them; there should be at least one of those characters between the brackets.
Examples:
[[BRAND]] -> Correct
[[ ]] -> Correct
[[]] -> Incorrect
2) Use your template! You have two ways of using a template: you can upload the template as a .txt file, or you can copy and paste it in the appropriate field.
The page will move to the output section, and the template will be ready to use.

3) Copy the results! The output section has two self-explanatory buttons. Once you've filled each field with whatever you want, click the first one to copy the results to your clipboard, or click on the second one to go back to the homepage to start over.
